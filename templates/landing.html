📖 About This Project

This platform manages the full lifecycle of graduation projects from proposals and supervision to final evaluations. Each user role is uniquely integrated to support collaboration and oversight.

🎓

Students

Submit proposals, receive supervisor feedback, track project milestones, and upload deliverables.

👨‍🏫

Supervisors

Guide students through the project, evaluate progress, and contribute to final grading.

📋

Coordinators

Oversee department activities, assign supervisors to students, and ensure smooth project workflow.

🛠️

Admins

Manage institutional structures like colleges and departments, and configure user roles and permissions.
